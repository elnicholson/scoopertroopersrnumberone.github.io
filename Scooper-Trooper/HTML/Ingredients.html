Our Premium Ingredients

Discover the unique and high-quality ingredients that make our ice cream a delightful treat for everyone.

Basic Ingredients

  • Milk: A variety of milks including whole, 2%, skim, and plant-based options such as almond, soy, and oat.
  • Cream: Cream is sourced from organically raised, grass-fed cows, ensuring a rich and creamy texture.
  • Sugar: Natural sweeteners including white cane sugar, beet sugar, honey, maple syrup, and agave nectar.
  • Egg Yolks: Custard-based ice creams use only free-range, pasteurised egg yolks.

Flavour Specific Ingredients

  • Vanilla: Extracted from premium Madagascar Bourbon, Mexican, and Tahitian vanilla beans.
  • Chocolate: Sourced from Ecuadorian dark chocolate and Belgian chocolates,
    available in forms like powder, syrup, and chunks.
  • Fruits: Fresh, frozen, or dried varieties like raspberries and strawberries.
  • Nuts: A selection of almonds, pistachios, and walnuts, available roasted or raw.

Specialty and Gourmet Ingredients

  • Superfoods: Ingredients like acai berries, chia seeds, and matcha add a nutritious punch.
  • Herbs and Spices: Natural herbs like mint, cinnamon, cardamom, and saffron.
  • Liqueurs and Extracts: For an adult twist, we incorporate flavours from rum, bourbon, and almond extract.
  • Artisan Components: Hand-made ice cream cakes and sandwiches, infused with fresh fruits
    and biscuits enchance your Scooper Troopers experience.

Allergen Information

We are transparent about the allergens present in our products.
All our items are labelled for allergens like nuts, dairy, soy, eggs, and gluten.
We take careful measures to prevent cross-contamination.

Vegan and Gluten-Free Options

Our Vegan products are made with plant-based milks and creams, and we ensure that our gluten-free offerings
are safe from cross-contamination.
We also provide detailed nutritional information for all our products.
